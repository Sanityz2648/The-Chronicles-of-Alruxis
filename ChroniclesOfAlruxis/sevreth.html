Sevreth

Sevreth is the first of the Primordials created by the Allfather. He is the Primordial of Time and is often referred to as "The Gardener of Time" by his fellow Primordials and his followers. Sevreth is responsible for maintaining the Timestream, a vessel for the flow of time and it many alternate universes. Due to his role and nature, he is very patient as well as calm and wise. He is considered to be one of the humblest of the Primordials due to his direct connection to time and it's inevitability. He is also one of the most powerful Primordials in terms of actual power. He can manipulate time in any way he seems fit, however he does no such thing as he considers the Timestream to be a sacred vessel that only needs the occasional pruning and maintenance when a timeline threatens the sanctity of the Timestream. He is well respected by his fellow Primordials and is often sought out for advice and guidance with the younger Primordials even referring to him as "Grandfather" due to his age and wisdom. His appearance is a major contributor to his title of "Grandfather Time".

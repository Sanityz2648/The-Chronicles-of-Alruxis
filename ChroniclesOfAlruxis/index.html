The Chronicles of Alruxis

The Chronicles of Alruxis is a comprehensive collection of stories, lore, and information about the many planets, galaxies, universes, and dimensions that make up the many creations of the Allfather. It preserves the lives of every being graced with life from his imagination, the history of every realm, the stories of the beings many consider heroes, the gods mortals look to for guidance and salvation, and those who seek to destroy the Allfather and all he has created. Every entry is just another piece of the Allfather's grand design, and eventual creation of utopia. The chronicles are created and maintained by a mortal turned god, Alruxis, the Divine Scribe, a mortal who was granted immortality and divinity due to his unwavering dedication to his chosen art form of literature. These chronicles are merely a work in progress as his work never truly ends, and with the Allfather isolated in his workshop, there will always only be more stories to tell, and more individuals to be preserved in these chronicles, created and preserved for eternity, by the Divine Scribe, Alruxis.

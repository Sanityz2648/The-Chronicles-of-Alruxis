The Allfather

                The Allfather or Father as he is known by the primordials, is the creator of all things.
                He is a member of the precursor race and he is amoung the elite in their society.
                He however, threw away his position, his power, and his name in order to leave and fufil
                his dream of creating a perfect utopia. With this, he left and created the first realm,
                the divine realm, and with it, he created the first of his creations, the Primordials.
                From this description you would think the Allfather was an ancient and wise being, however
                he is a mear child in terms of Precursors. Even if he were to grow, the curse placed on his as firstborn
                of the Owners of Creation would prevent his appearance from changing much if at all.

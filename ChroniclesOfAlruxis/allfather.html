The Allfather

The Allfather or Father as he is known by the Primordials, is the creator of all things. He is a member of the precursor race and he is among the elite in their society. He however, threw away his position, his power, and his name in order to leave and fulfill his dream of creating a perfect utopia. With this, he left and created the first realm, the divine realm, and with it, he created the first of his creations, the Primordials. From this description you would think the Allfather wasn't an ancient and wise being, however he is a mere child in terms of Precursors. Even if he were to grow, the curse placed on his as firstborn of the Owners of Creation would prevent his appearance from changing much if at all. He now spend most of his time in his workshop, creating and building, trying to create his perfect utopia and the creations that will live within.
